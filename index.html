About IDEAS and STARTUP PURV

IDEAS and STARTUP PURV is a public-focused initiative dedicated to fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and digital empowerment.

The initiative connects students, startups, professionals, and community members with opportunities, guidance, and a strong innovation-driven ecosystem.
